Where to find the model number.

The quickest way to get the correct replacement is to send the brand plus the model from the unit or the old remote.

Most TV model numbers are on a sticker on the rear panel or side edge.
Air conditioner model labels are often on the indoor unit side or under the front flap.

For TVs

Check the back of the TV first. If it’s easier, send the model printed on the old remote as well.

  • 1Look for the product sticker on the rear panel or side edge of the TV.
  • 2Copy the full model code, not just the screen size.
  • 3If you still have the old remote, its model number can help confirm the match.
